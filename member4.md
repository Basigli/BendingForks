# Member 4 - Adozione & Integratore Finale

## Project Component

You are the lead for Change Management and the overall Project Integrator. Your focus is ensuring smooth employee onboarding, decommissioning old apps, handling the Steering Committee, and stitching the final PM document together.

## Tasks (Workstream Specific)

- [ ] Draft the **RBS (User Stories)** and **WBS branch** for employee training, onboarding, and system switch-over.
- [ ] Draft the **Risk Analysis** for poor user adoption and the limited time availability of the Steering Committee.
- [ ] Co-author the POS/PDS focusing on your component's goals.

## Tasks (Shared PM Deliverables)

- [ ] Merge the team's WBS branches to create the single cohesive **Gantt Chart**.
- [ ] Draft the theoretical approach for the **Closing** phase.
- [ ] Assemble the final **Descrizione dell'approccio utilizzato** document, ensuring all PM choices are well-justified.
- [ ] Create the final index of all attached materials.

## Workflow & Pipeline

1. **Scoping Phase (Initiation):** Define the requirements for user adoption, training, and decommissioning of old systems. Contribute your objectives to the POS and PDS. Draft the RBS (User Stories) for change management and onboarding.

2. **Planning Phase:** Create your WBS branch for adoption tasks. Assess risks related to user resistance and Steering Committee availability, drafting your part of the Risk Analysis.
3. **Integration & Collaboration (Continuous):** Act as the central hub. Collect the WBS branches from Members 1, 2, and 3 to assemble the unified project Gantt chart. Ensure consistency and alignment across all individual contributions.
4. **Execution & Closure:** Draft the theoretical approach for the Closing phase. Finally, compile all project documents, write the overarching "Descrizione dell'approccio utilizzato", and create the final index of attachments to deliver the completed PM project.
