# Member 3 - Moduli AI & Ricerca

## Project Component

You are the lead for the AI search automation. Your focus is training and fine-tuning the local AI models to retrieve documents quickly, solving the "latency" and "search difficulty" problems of BendingForks.

## Tasks (Workstream Specific)

- [ ] Draft the **RBS (User Stories)** and **WBS branch** for AI model selection, training, testing, and integration.
- [ ] Draft the **Risk Analysis** for AI hallucinations, slow search queries, and potential API leaks.
- [ ] Co-author the POS/PDS focusing on your component's goals.

## Tasks (Shared PM Deliverables)

- [ ] Draft the theoretical approach for the **Execution (Launching)** phase.
- [ ] Draft the theoretical approach for the **Monitoring & Controlling** phase.

## Workflow & Pipeline

1. **Scoping Phase (Initiation):** Define the capabilities required for the local AI models. Contribute your goals to the POS and PDS. Develop the RBS (User Stories) covering AI model selection, localized training, testing, and system integration.

2. **Planning Phase:** Break your RBS down into a detailed WBS branch. Identify AI-specific risks, such as hallucinations, query latency, and data leakage, and draft your section of the Risk Analysis.
3. **Integration & Collaboration:** Coordinate with Member 1 to secure the necessary on-premise compute resources for AI training, and with Member 2 to ensure seamless integration of the AI search into the ECM platform. Provide your WBS branch to Member 4 for the project Gantt chart.
4. **Execution & Closure:** Lead the drafting of the theoretical approach for the Launching/Execution and Monitoring & Controlling phases, detailing how the project will be tracked and managed once implementation begins.
