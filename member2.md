# Member 2 - Piattaforma ECM & Migrazione Dati

## Project Component

You are the lead for the core Enterprise Content Management system. Your focus is selecting/configuring the ECM software, mapping the old databases, and migrating documents without data loss.

## Tasks (Workstream Specific)

- [ ] Draft the **RBS (User Stories)** and **WBS branch** for ECM selection, configuration, and data migration.
- [ ] Draft the **Risk Analysis** for data loss during migration, poor interoperability, and system crashes.
- [ ] Co-author the POS/PDS focusing on your component's goals.

## Tasks (Shared PM Deliverables)

- [ ] Plan and document the **JPPS (Joint Project Planning Session)** meeting (Agenda, Participants, Summary).

## Workflow & Pipeline

1. **Scoping Phase (Initiation):** Start by identifying the requirements for the ECM platform and data migration. Provide your objectives for the POS and PDS. Create the RBS (User Stories) detailing software selection, configuration, and the migration process.

2. **Planning Phase:** Translate your RBS into a detailed WBS branch. Analyze risks specific to your domain, such as data loss during migration or interoperability issues, and draft your part of the Risk Analysis. Lead the planning and documentation of the JPPS meeting.
3. **Integration & Collaboration:** Work with Member 1 to ensure the infrastructure meets ECM requirements, and with Member 3 to provide the necessary integration points for the AI search modules. Hand over your WBS branch to Member 4 for the overall Gantt chart.
4. **Execution & Closure:** Assist Member 3 with ECM-specific details for the Execution and Monitoring phases, and provide final migration reports to Member 4 for the Closing phase.
