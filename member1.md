# Member 1 - Infrastruttura & Sicurezza

## Project Component

You are the lead for the On-Premise/Hybrid Infrastructure setup and Data Privacy enforcement. Your focus is ensuring that "BendingForks" servers are reliable and that no financial data ever touches public AI APIs.

## Tasks (Workstream Specific)

- [ ] Draft the **RBS (User Stories)** and **WBS branch** for infrastructure setup and security audits.
- [ ] Draft the **Risk Analysis** for data breaches, server downtime, and latency issues.
- [ ] Co-author the POS/PDS focusing on your component's goals.

## Tasks (Shared PM Deliverables)

- [ ] Document the **PMLC (Project Management Life Cycle)** choice, justifying it academically.
- [ ] Write the summary and minutes for the **Project Scoping Meeting** (Agenda, Participants, Outcomes).

## Workflow & Pipeline

1. **Scoping Phase (Initiation):** Begin by establishing the infrastructure and security baselines. Contribute your specific objectives to the POS and PDS. Translate these into an RBS (User Stories) focused on server setup and security audits. Simultaneously, take charge of documenting the Project Scoping Meeting and academically justifying the team's PMLC choice.

2. **Planning Phase:** Decompose your RBS into specific, manageable tasks to form your WBS branch. Evaluate potential threats (e.g., data breaches, server downtime) to draft your section of the Risk Analysis.
3. **Integration & Collaboration:** Coordinate closely with Member 2 to ensure the infrastructure supports the ECM, and with Member 3 to guarantee sufficient compute for local AI models without exposing data. Submit your completed WBS to Member 4 for the master Gantt chart integration.
4. **Execution & Closure:** Support Member 3 and Member 4 by providing infrastructure metrics and documentation for the Execution, Monitoring, and Closing phases.
